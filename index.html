Welcome to Minority dice!

Minority Dice is a true-random dice DAPP in the Ethereum blockchain.
Results of each round is determined by players.

How to play:

A dice is rolled and players choose between selections
BIG or SMALL

The result of the dice is determined by the total amount wagered on each selection.
The choice of the majority loses to the minority.
Players from the winning selection will share the whole amount collected from the losing selection.
